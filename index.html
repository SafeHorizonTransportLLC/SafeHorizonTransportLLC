Mission Statement

SafeHorizon Transport LLC provides compassionate, accessible, and reliable transportation for individuals with mobility challenges in Southern California. With modern, wheelchair-accessible vehicles and a caring team, we support independence, safety, and well-being on every ride—while also promoting sustainability for a better tomorrow. At SafeHorizon Transport, every journey is a step toward freedom and connection.

Our Services

Wheelchair Accessible Rides

Spacious, ADA-compliant vehicles for individuals with mobility needs.

Medical Appointments

On-time transportation for recurring or one-time medical visits.

Community Events

Safe and courteous rides to recreational and community outings.

Coordinated Family Support Services

Personalized service coordination for families living together, addressing support gaps.
